Congratulations on being a Tatchi customer!

Tatchi is a strategy game platform that does not rely on vision but instead relies on a combination of memory, hearing, and sense of touch.

Instructions:

To begin, install 4 AA batteries in your Tatchi device. Turn on Tatchi by pushing the power switch. Use the arrow keys to select the game of your choice. Tatchi comes preloaded with one game, Blank Space. Enter the number of players and number of tiles in play and the device will read out the game’s instructions. These can be skipped by using the right arrow, or replayed by pressing the left arrow. Follow the instructions to begin game play!

To begin another round of the same game with the same number of players and tiles, hit the middle shuffle button and the tiles will automatically be reassigned random values. To play another game, or change one of the game parameters, push the off switch, turn the device back on, and repeat the aforementioned process.

To download new games to the device, connect Tatchi to a computer using the USB cable and download games from Tatchi’s ecosystem of products.
